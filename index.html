About Us

Eco-Leaf is a youth-driven climate action and conservation NGO founded in 2025 by environmentalist Edgar Kamau in Kenya. Born out of the urgent need to address deforestation, biodiversity loss, and climate change impacts, we work at the intersection of community action, education, and environmental restoration. Our mission is to protect Kenya’s natural heritage while empowering local communities and young people to become active stewards of the environment.

Our Values

  • Sustainability
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
  • Innovation

By bridging knowledge gaps and empowering action, Eco-Leaf aims to create resilient ecosystems and communities that thrive in harmony with nature.At the heart of our work is a holistic approach that integrates science, community engagement, and policy influence.

Services

What We Do

Eco-Leaf offers a range of practical services and programs designed to drive real environmental and social impact across Kenya.We combine education, hands-on action, research, and advocacy to create lasting change.
  • Climate Awareness Campaigns
  • Environmental Conservation Projects
  • Biodiversity Protection Programs
  • Community Empowerment and Training

Our Projects

Green Roots Initiative

Large-scale reforestation and land restoration program focused on planting indigenous trees and training communities in sustainable agroforestry.

Leaf Legacy Biodiversity Project

Protecting Kenya’s rich biodiversity through habitat restoration, wildlife monitoring, and community-based conservation efforts.

Sustainable Rivers & Waters Initiative

Restoring Kenya’s rivers, wetlands, and water catchments through clean-up drives, riverbank reforestation, and sustainable water management training.
